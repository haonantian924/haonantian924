## 🚜 Building Reliable Edge AI for Agriculture

I am the **Founder of SRD AI**, currently incubated at **Opus, University of Saskatchewan**.

At SRD AI, I am building a **reliability layer for agricultural edge AI** — helping AI models move from clean demos into real machines, real fields, and harsh outdoor environments.

Many agricultural AI systems can perform well in the lab, but fail in the field because of camera dropouts, heat, vibration, power instability, software crashes, GPU hangs, and limited on-site technical support.

**SRD AI focuses on one core problem:**

> Turning fragile AI prototypes into reliable, deployable field systems.

---

## 🧩 What SRD AI Is Building

SRD AI combines **standardized model deployment**, **edge device monitoring**, **local recovery**, and **failure traceability** for field-deployed AI systems.

### Core Capabilities

- **Standardized Edge AI Deployment**  
  Packaging and deploying AI models onto edge devices such as NVIDIA Jetson and other embedded AI platforms.

- **Reliability Monitoring**  
  Tracking device health, camera input, inference status, temperature, power behavior, GPU/DLA activity, and software failures.

- **Local Recovery Layer**  
  Using watchdogs, service restart, tiered recovery, power-cycle control, and fallback logic to keep AI systems running without constant human intervention.

- **Black-Box Failure Traceability**  
  Recording what happened before, during, and after field failures so OEMs and AI teams can debug problems without guessing.

- **Agricultural Field Focus**  
  Targeting harsh, unattended, and low-maintenance environments such as farm equipment, outdoor robots, smart implements, and rugged AI devices.
